Scope

Worked alongside design, operations and maintenance and construction engineers as well as architects over the 2024 summer and for the 2024 fall semester. I also had the opportunity to communicate within departments to ensure we had the approval to enter some of the older buildings safely and with proper authorization.

My first projects involved communicating with a previous intern on updating chilled water schematics. This project introduced me to surveying buildings and retracing piping to update/develop schematics for chilled water or condensed air piping. This involved over 50+ different chilled water drawings of various buildings

Some other projects that I got to work on were the fire damper project and the mechanical riser plan. Both involved updating old schematics that were out of date and needed to be revised. For the fire damper project we surveyed the building and traced fire dampers for the purpose of ensuring that the building was up to the safety codes. For the mechanical riser we had schematics that display the most up to date piping, plumbing and assets that are located to each respective building. This involved going through various projects in chronological order and ensuring that any construction or demolition was reflected on the schematic.
